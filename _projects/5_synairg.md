As a Research Scientist on the EU Horizon project
[**SynAir-G**](https://www.synairg.eu/) (Grant Agreement No. 101057271), I investigate
synergistic interactions between indoor air quality metrics and physiological signals from
wearable devices, using multivariate and temporal causal models to study their combined
impact on childhood health and wellbeing.
