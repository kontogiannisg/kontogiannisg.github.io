Under a research contract with the Computer Technology Institute and Press "Diophantus"
(**CTI**), I develop ML/DL systems to optimize the nationwide school textbook distribution
ecosystem under Greece's new multiple-textbook policy. This includes predictive models for
student population, textbook demand, and inventory forecasting; clustering of schools by
textbook-selection patterns; and simulation of logistics and what-if supply-chain
scenarios, integrated into the Publications Directorate IT infrastructure.
