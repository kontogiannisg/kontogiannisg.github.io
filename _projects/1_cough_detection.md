Designed, developed, and deployed smartphone-based services for cough detection,
respiratory disease analysis, and continuous screening using tracheal-acquired lung
sound recordings. The pipeline runs in real time on commodity Android hardware using
the built-in microphone, with abnormal/normal lung-sound classification and diagnostic
models that distinguish asthma from COPD based on acoustic biomarkers and patient
clinical history.

Developed under the externally funded **Pfizer Collaborative Development Initiative
(CDI)** Voice-Based Diagnostics contracts (VBD-1 and VBD-2) at the University of Patras.
